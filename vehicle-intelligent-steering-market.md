<p><p>The Vehicle Intelligent Steering Market encompasses two primary technologies: Electric Power Steering (EPS) and Steering-By-Wire (SBW). EPS enhances steering responsiveness and fuel efficiency by using electric motors for assistance, adapting to driving conditions. In contrast, SBW eliminates mechanical links by electronically controlling steering inputs, allowing for greater design flexibility and advanced features like customizable steering feel. These innovations contribute to improved vehicle handling, safety, and driver comfort, reflecting the shift towards smart, connected automotive solutions.</p></p>
