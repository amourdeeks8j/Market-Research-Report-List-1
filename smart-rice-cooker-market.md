<p><p>The smart rice cooker market comprises various types, including traditional rice cookers and induction heating (IH) rice cookers. Traditional rice cookers utilize basic heating methods, often relying on a simple on/off thermostat to cook rice effectively. In contrast, IH rice cookers employ advanced induction heating technology, providing precise temperature control and even cooking through electromagnetic induction. This results in improved texture and flavor, catering to varying preferences. Both types serve unique consumer needs, driving growth in the smart rice cooker market.</p></p>
