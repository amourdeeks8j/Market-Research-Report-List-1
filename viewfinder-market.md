<p><strong>The Viewfinder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Viewfinder</li><li>Electronic Viewfinder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The viewfinder market is primarily divided into two types: optical viewfinders (OVF) and electronic viewfinders (EVF). Optical viewfinders, commonly found in DSLR cameras, provide a direct optical pathway using mirrors and prisms, offering a real-time, lag-free experience with a natural view of the scene. In contrast, electronic viewfinders use digital displays to present the camera's sensor feed, allowing for features like exposure previews and overlays. This distinction caters to varying user preferences and technological advancements in photography.</p></p>
