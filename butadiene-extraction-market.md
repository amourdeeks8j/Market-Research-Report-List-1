<p><p>The Butadiene extraction market encompasses various methodologies for isolating this valuable compound. C4 hydrocarbons, sourced from steam crackers, serve as a primary feedstock for butadiene production. Meanwhile, the dehydrogenation of n-butane transforms hydrocarbons through thermal processes to yield butadiene. Additionally, ethanol can be converted into butadiene via dehydration and dehydrogenation processes. Lastly, butenes offer another pathway, as they undergo dehydrogenation to produce butadiene, showcasing the diversity of technologies employed in this sector.</p></p>
