<p><p>The medical packaging paper market comprises two main types: coated and uncoated paper. Coated paper is treated with a special coating, enhancing its barrier properties against moisture, bacteria, and gases, making it ideal for sterile packaging. In contrast, uncoated paper, while less resistant, offers breathability and is often used for wrapping or protecting medical instruments. Both types are crucial for ensuring product integrity and compliance with safety standards in healthcare, addressing the diverse needs of the medical industry.</p></p>
