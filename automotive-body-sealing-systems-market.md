<p><p>The automotive body sealing systems market comprises various materials, including EPDM, PVC, and TPE sealing systems. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) offers excellent weather resistance, ideal for sealing edges and preventing water ingress. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is cost-effective and flexible, often used for window and door seals. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) combines the elasticity of rubber with the processing advantages of thermoplastics, providing effective sealing and enhanced durability. Each material serves specific applications within vehicle construction, contributing to overall performance and longevity.</p></p>
