<p><p>The smartphone audio codecs market is divided into two main types: lossless compression and lossy compression. Lossless compression retains the original audio quality, making it ideal for audiophiles and professionals who prioritize sound fidelity. In contrast, lossy compression reduces file size by discarding some audio data, sacrificing quality for efficiency, which appeals to casual listeners and streaming services. Both types cater to different user preferences and applications, shaping the overall landscape of mobile audio experiences.</p></p>
