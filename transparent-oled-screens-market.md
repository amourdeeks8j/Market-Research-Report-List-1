<p><strong>The Transparent OLED Screens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMOLED Screens</li><li>PMOLED Screens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transparent OLED screen market encompasses two primary types: AMOLED and PMOLED screens. AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED) screens utilize a thin-film transistor for each pixel, allowing for faster pixel response and higher refresh rates, making them ideal for dynamic content. PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) screens, on the other hand, have a simpler structure with lower production costs, but they offer slower refresh rates and are best suited for simpler displays. Both types leverage advanced technology for transparency and visual quality in various applications.</p></p>
