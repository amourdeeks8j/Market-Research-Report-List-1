<p><p>The insomnia therapeutics drugs market includes several categories. Benzodiazepines are sedative-hypnotics that enhance GABA activity, providing quick relief but potential addiction. Non-benzodiazepines offer similar effects with a lower risk of dependence. Antidepressants, particularly certain SSRIs and SNRIs, help with insomnia linked to depression. Orexin antagonists block wakefulness-promoting neuropeptides for sleep regulation. Melatonin antagonists work on the body’s sleep-wake cycle, while other agents encompass various sleep aids and herbal remedies, expanding treatment options for insomnia.</p></p>
