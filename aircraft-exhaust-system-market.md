<p><strong>The Aircraft Exhaust System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Stack System</li><li>Collector System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft exhaust system market comprises primarily two types: short stack systems and collector systems. Short stack systems feature individual exhaust outlets for each cylinder, providing a straightforward design that enhances performance in smaller aircraft. In contrast, collector systems consolidate exhaust from multiple cylinders into a single outlet, offering improved efficiency and reduced noise levels. Both systems cater to different aircraft needs, with short stacks often used in light aircraft and collector systems prevalent in larger, more complex aircraft designs.</p></p>
