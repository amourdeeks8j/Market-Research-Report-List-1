<p><p>Intelligent Video Analytics (IVA) systems are categorized into camera-based and server-based systems. Camera-based systems integrate analytics directly within the surveillance cameras, enabling real-time processing and immediate response capabilities at the source. In contrast, server-based systems utilize centralized servers to analyze video feeds from multiple cameras, allowing for more sophisticated analysis and storage. Both systems enhance security and operational efficiency by leveraging advanced algorithms for tasks like object detection, motion tracking, and behavior analysis, catering to diverse surveillance needs.</p></p>
