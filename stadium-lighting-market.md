<p><strong>The Stadium Lighting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-intensity discharge (HID)</li><li>Light-emitting diode (LED)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stadium lighting market primarily includes two types: High-Intensity Discharge (HID) and Light-Emitting Diode (LED) lighting. HID lamps, known for their high brightness and energy efficiency, have traditionally been used in large venues. However, they have longer warm-up times and shorter lifespans. In contrast, LED lighting offers superior energy efficiency, instant illumination, and longer operational lifespans. LEDs are increasingly preferred for their adaptability, lower maintenance costs, and ability to produce varied lighting effects, enhancing the overall spectator experience.</p></p>
