<p><p>4D printing in healthcare involves the use of advanced materials and technologies to create responsive medical devices that can adapt over time. Key market types include Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM), which extrudes thermoplastic filaments; PolyJet, which uses inkjet technology to print layers of photopolymer; Stereolithography (SLA), utilizing ultraviolet light to cure resin; and Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), which fuses powdered materials with a laser. Each technology offers unique benefits for creating customized, programmable healthcare solutions.</p></p>
