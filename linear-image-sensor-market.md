<p><p>The linear image sensor market comprises several types, including CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor), CCD (Charge-Coupled Device), and other technologies. CMOS sensors are favored for their low power consumption, faster readout speeds, and integration capabilities, making them ideal for mobile applications. CCD sensors are known for their high image quality and sensitivity, particularly in low-light conditions, commonly used in professional cameras. Other types may include hybrid sensors or specialty devices tailored for niche applications, enhancing overall market diversity and innovation.</p></p>
