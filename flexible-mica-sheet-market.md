<p><p>The flexible mica sheet market primarily consists of two types: phlogopite mica and muscovite mica. Phlogopite mica, known for its high thermal stability and excellent electrical insulation properties, is often used in high-temperature applications. Muscovite mica, on the other hand, is more widely utilized due to its availability and good dielectric properties, making it suitable for general electrical applications. Both types are essential in industries such as electronics, automotive, and construction, where heat resistance and insulation are crucial.</p></p>
