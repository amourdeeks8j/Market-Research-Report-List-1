<p><p>The Pressure Relief Damper market can be categorized into two main types based on their mounting orientation: vertical mount and horizontal mount. Vertical mount dampers are designed to be installed in a vertical position, typically used in applications with limited horizontal space, allowing for efficient air flow management in tall structures. In contrast, horizontal mount dampers are installed horizontally, suitable for conventional ductwork systems, enhancing air pressure control and balancing in commercial and industrial environments.</p></p>
