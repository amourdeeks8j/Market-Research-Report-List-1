<p><p>The cosmetic pigments market is primarily categorized into two types: inorganic and organic pigments. Inorganic pigments are derived from mineral sources and offer durability, opacity, and lightfastness, making them ideal for foundations and color cosmetics. They are often non-toxic and stable. Conversely, organic pigments are synthesized from carbon-based compounds, known for their vibrant colors and versatility. These pigments tend to provide brighter hues but may lack the permanence of inorganic options. Both types cater to diverse cosmetic formulations and consumer preferences.</p></p>
