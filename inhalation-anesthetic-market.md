<p><p>The inhalation anesthetic market comprises several key types: Sevoflurane, Isoflurane, Desflurane, and others. Sevoflurane is popular for its rapid onset and minimal side effects, making it ideal for various surgeries, especially in pediatrics. Isoflurane is favored for its stability and analgesic properties in critical care. Desflurane, known for rapid recovery, is often used in outpatient procedures. Other inhalation agents include nitrous oxide and halothane, each with specific applications and benefits in anesthesia practices worldwide.</p></p>
