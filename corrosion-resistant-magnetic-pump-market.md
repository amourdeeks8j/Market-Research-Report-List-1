<p><p>Corrosion-resistant magnetic pumps are designed to handle corrosive fluids without leakage, using a modular design that includes either stationary or rotating shafts. </p><p>Stationary shaft magnetic pumps use a fixed outer magnet to drive an inner impeller magnet, ensuring no direct contact with the motor, which minimizes wear. Rotating shaft magnetic pumps, in contrast, feature both magnets rotating together, providing smoother operation and increased efficiency. Both types serve various industries, such as chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment, enhancing reliability and performance.</p></p>
